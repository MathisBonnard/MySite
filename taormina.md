# Description 

Taormina is a beautifull old city between sea and mountains. You might be lucky enought and see the continental part of Italy (<i>La Calabria</i>).

The white waters of the sea surrond an island called <i>Isola Bella</i>.

The place is one of the rare city where you can find luxury goods and hotels: it's a very hyped place among the richs.

You might have heard about this city if you've seen the second season of White Lotus because the story takes places in... Taormina of course!

<br>

# How to get there

Since the city is a bit high, I do not recommand taking the train. Regional busses can bring you there.

Departure are every 30 (or 15!) minutes and you don't have to take off the bus before the last stop. There is around 1 hour of bus, but it can get worse depending on the traffic...
The trip is offered by either interbus or Etna trasporti and cost around 5€ one way.


<br>

# What to see and visit

From the bus terminal, continue on the way up, following the flow of tourists. When arrived in the city center, take all you time to enjoy the narrow streets as well as the main alley!

The ancient theater is very nice to visit with the Etna that can be seen in the background. The entrance ticket is 16€.

If you are in a good shape and are lucky enough to have a clear view on the sea, I advise you to climb up in the direction of [the castle](https://maps.app.goo.gl/15iaQMvgKitBZTuC9).

Take a tour in the public park [<i>Villa Comunale di Taornìmina</i>](https://maps.app.goo.gl/DEmGjwC2Lj7ZSB7b6)


If you want to spend even more time in this nice area, you can take the cable car (it's not far from the bus terminal) to go at the sea level. From there you can either spend time on the stone beach or go on the <i>Isola Bella</i>. Round trip is 10€ using the cable car.
