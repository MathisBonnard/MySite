# Description 

Taormina is a beautifull old city between sea and mountains. You might be lucky enought and see the continental part of Italy (<it> La Calabria </it>).

The white waters of the sea surrond an island called <it> Isola Bella </it>.

The place is one of the rare city where you can find luxury goods and hotels: it's a very hyped place among the richs.

You might have heard about this city if you've seen the second season of White Lotus because the story takes places in... Taormina of course!

<br>

# How to get there

Since the city is a bit high, I do not recommand taking the train. Regional busses can bring you there.

Departure are every 30 (or 15!) minutes and you don't have to take off the bus before the last stop.
The trip is offered by either interbus or Etna trasporti and cost around 5€ one way.
