Since i'll be with you for the eating part in Catania, it should be fine. But I write here some snack tips and good-to-know stuff.

<br>
# What to eat

First of all, what you SHOULD try while you're here. It's an important question because Sicily isn't only pasta and pizza (but don't worry, you will have some!)

Sicily has developped a very unique eat-on-the-go dish which is <b>arrancino</b>. It's a rice-based ball with something inside (butter, ham, ragu, pistachio, etc) and then deep fried. It's very goos and quite inexpensive: you find them for 2,5 - 3€ and with two of them you're full!

Regarding restaurants, we have plenty of <i>trattoria</i> which serve pasta and meat (especially horse meat in Catania) for a pretty low price. A very nice place to have a 15 people dinner on saturday night and drink as much as you eat (ie. too much maybe).

The sweet-side of food brings us <i>cannoli</i> which are basically ricotta cheese in a deep-fried shell. You definitely have to try our amazing <i>granita</i> which are very different from what you can find elsewhere (even in Italy); I particularly like them with a brioche!

<br>
# Where to have a snack

Easy: EVERYWHERE.

For ice creams, go to [Don Peppinu](https://maps.app.goo.gl/hCbpAjmo6VWTG7Lt7): it's not the best ice cream but it's fairly good and right in the center. I also really like [COCHI](https://maps.app.goo.gl/4Xn4EFyGYYypfPRz7). But anywhere else is also very nice: I don't remember ever being disappointed with an ice cream.

The best granita in Catania is at [Comis Ice Cafè](https://maps.app.goo.gl/Ja5XXiZApfvKqoCX9) but if you go there, please text me because I also want to have mine!

I you want a salty snack, go to the nearest <i>panificio</i> or <i>tavola calda</i>: you'll propably find something that feed you (eg: the arracncini).

<br>
# And a proper restaurant?

Plenty of choice for this one!

For a pizza, [Squid](https://maps.app.goo.gl/DnWTUJubQVFJQzef9), [Rasoterra](https://maps.app.goo.gl/G8EsZy6sUYTqWYgi8) or [Acido Lattico](https://maps.app.goo.gl/TDM8k69rSGCKQ5W97) are among my favourites. But I've been pretty happy with all the pizza I've been to!

The best trattoria is obviously the [Trattoria del Cavaliere](https://maps.app.goo.gl/viLRkXpXYdybJzPb7) or maybe [Re Carlo V](https://maps.app.goo.gl/jaLMcryMpUtCXuKE9).

For more conventionnal restaurant, [FUD](https://maps.app.goo.gl/vY3Uw1QaJMRwC2iEA) and [Curtigghiu](https://maps.app.goo.gl/5mHXgR9mAyz6uFKe8) are good choices.

<br>
# Where to graba beer or a cocktail

My friday and saturday HQ is clearly at the [Glamour](https://maps.app.goo.gl/rvXhVLTjF2AHrTcm7). Sometimes, however we go to [Mezzaparola](https://maps.app.goo.gl/pVBiLsWbrCNDhWQq6) or [Mosaik](https://maps.app.goo.gl/uYUT2Dnepg5AdkoG9).

The [Vermut](https://maps.app.goo.gl/Qn45gPVghrSajqzH8) is probably one of the most hyped places for students here and is full of people ont the weekends!
